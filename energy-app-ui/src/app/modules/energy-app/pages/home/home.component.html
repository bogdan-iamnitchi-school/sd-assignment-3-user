Welcome to ENERGY-APP

Monitor and manage your energy consumption with ease! Our ENERGY-APP provides real-time analytics to help you track usage, optimize your energy costs, and make informed decisions. With user-friendly controls and alerts, you can effortlessly maintain control over your energy consumption.

Features

  • Real-time monitoring of devices
  • Energy usage statistics
  • Notifications and alerts
  • User-friendly interface

How It Works

Our application connects to your devices, allowing you to track energy consumption in real-time. You can receive alerts for unusual activity, set usage limits, and get insights into your energy usage patterns.
